Our Beginning

Our Story

Plant Palace was born from a simple belief: every home deserves a touch of green. Founded in Solapur, Maharashtra, we started as a small local nursery and have grown into a trusted online plant destination serving thousands of happy plant lovers across India.

Every plant we sell is personally selected, nurtured, and quality-checked by our expert horticulturists. We believe that buying a plant should be a joyful, educational, and environmentally positive experience.
